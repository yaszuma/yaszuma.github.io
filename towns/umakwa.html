Umakwa

Umakwa ("Holy City" or "High City" in Yaszumatti) is a sky island chain and the current capital city of the Crownlands of Yaszuma. It's the largest chain of sky islands in Eldham and and also one of the five provinces of Yaszuma.


Location

We are located at the Northwest part of Eldham.
